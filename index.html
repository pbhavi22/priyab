Who
I am

The person

Hi, I'm Priya

I'm a recent Penn grad now living in New York City, figuring out who I want to be at the intersection of people, creativity, and systems. I care about community, about what gets documented and what gets left out, and about making things that feel genuinely alive.

I grew up drawn to photographs and stories — the ones that reveal something true about a moment or a person. That's still what drives most of what I make and most of what I notice.

New York City University of Pennsylvania '26 Photographer Videographer Designer Beginner potter

Right now

Currently

Starting as a Client Service Associate at AlphaSights in July. Getting lost in New York neighborhoods. Learning to center clay. Shooting everything that catches the light wrong.

What I believe

Things that
matter to me

Community spaces deserve more attention than they get. The best photographs happen when someone forgets the camera is there. Slow things are usually more interesting than fast things.

I find myself most alive when I'm making something with my hands — whether that's a photograph, a video, a piece of clay, or a page of HTML. The medium matters less than the intention behind it.
